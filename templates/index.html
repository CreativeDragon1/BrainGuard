Classification Guide

Non-Demented

Normal cognitive function. No signs of dementia detected in brain imaging.

Very Mild Dementia

Minimal cognitive changes. Early stage with minimal impact on daily activities.

Mild Dementia

Noticeable cognitive decline. Moderate brain changes visible in imaging.

Moderate Dementia

Significant cognitive impairment. Substantial brain atrophy visible.
